About Us

I Repair it:Is a company that provides home repair services to underserved communities in the townships, informal settlements and rural areas, with dignity, efficiency, dedication and skill.

At I Repair it, we are committed to delivering high-quality repair services that meet the unique needs of our community. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to providing reliable, affordable solutions for all your home repair needs.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers based on trust, transparency, and exceptional service. We are here to serve you with excellence.
